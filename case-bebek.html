Case Study

Bebek - AI Workflow Assistants

Senior PM leading an AI workflow automation platform for enterprise ops. Reduced manual toil with guarded GPT chains, accelerated activation via API‑first integrations, and converted pilots with usage‑based pricing.

Manual Work−2,000+ hrs/mo
SLA Compliance+35%
ARR (6 mo)$480K

Context

  • Ops teams managed high‑volume, repetitive tasks across support, onboarding, and QA.
  • Leaders piloted AI assistants but lacked measurable ROI to scale.

Constraints

  • Data sensitivity, need for auditability, and varied tooling (Slack/CRM/Calendar).
  • Outcomes over output: pilots had to show real time saved and SLA lift.

Strategy

  • Design guarded GPT chains with human‑in‑the‑loop (HITL) for critical steps.
  • Ship API‑first integrations to compress activation from weeks to days.

Key Decisions

  • Prompt library + safeguards (validation, fallbacks) to reduce hallucinations.
  • Usage‑based pricing with quotas; meter events to tie value to spend.
  • Telemetry for activation funnels; cohort views for WAU/retention.

Implementation

  • Slack/Calendar/CRM connectors; idempotent jobs and retries for reliability.
  • Role‑aware onboarding; templated workflows to scale across teams.

Results

  • 2,000+ hrs/month eliminated; SLA +35%.
  • Time‑to‑value 14 → 5 days; 7 pilots → 3 paid ($480K ARR/6 mo).
  • Setup time −50%; weekly active teams +45%.

Artifacts: Prompt catalog, workflow specs, activation dashboard, pricing sheet.
