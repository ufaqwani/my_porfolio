Case Study

Siemens Healthineers - AI Rad Companion

Product Manager owning diagnostic dashboard lifecycle across 15+ hospitals. Balanced regulatory requirements with clinician‑friendly UX, strengthened observability, and improved adoption.

Images/Week50K+
Triage Time−30%
Downtime/Incident−2 hrs

Context

  • Multi‑site deployments needed trustworthy AI outputs surfaced in clinician workflows.
  • Regulatory (FDA/CE) alignment was essential for launch and renewals.

Constraints

  • Strict audit trails, validation plans, and risk controls.
  • High availability requirements across diverse IT environments.

Strategy

  • Codify requirements with regulatory and AI/ML partners; make them testable.
  • Invest in observability and incident playbooks to reduce MTTR.

Key Decisions

  • Role‑based onboarding; permissions mapped to clinical responsibilities.
  • Golden signals and alerts; unified runbooks for on‑call teams.

Implementation

  • Real‑time pipelines processing 50K+ images/week with safe fallback paths.
  • Validation plans, audit logs, and release checklists aligned to FDA/CE.

Results

  • −30% triage time; −2 hrs downtime/incident.
  • +25% adoption; enabled multi‑million renewals across 5 health systems.

Artifacts: Risk matrix, validation plan, incident runbooks, onboarding flows.
