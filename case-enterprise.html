Case Study

IndianSmart Hub - Enterprise SaaS Platform

Product Manager scaling a multi‑tenant SaaS to 30+ institutions with role‑based UX and workflow modules for onboarding, surveys, and progress tracking.

Support Tickets−40%
Resolution Time−3 hrs
Upsell+25%

Context

  • Institutions needed configurable workflows and clear visibility for admins and end‑users.
  • Growth depended on smooth onboarding and measurable engagement.

Constraints

  • Multi‑tenant setup with varied roles and permissions.
  • Limited support bandwidth; self‑serve UX was key.

Strategy

  • Ship workflow modules (onboarding, surveys, progress) and define role maps.
  • Instrument activation and engagement; tune nudges and empty states.

Key Decisions

  • Role‑aware navigation and dashboards for admins vs. end‑users.
  • Templates for repeatable deployments; checklists to unblock setup.

Implementation

  • Progress tracking with alerts; survey flows with response analytics.
  • Guided onboarding with milestones and contextual tips.

Results

  • Support tickets −40%; resolution time −3 hrs.
  • Adoption across roles improved; 25% upsell to premium plans.

Artifacts: Role maps, onboarding checklist, engagement dashboard.
